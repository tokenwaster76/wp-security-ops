💡 The Positioning Gap

Wordfence Response at ~€100/mo is the most expensive productized WP security service. It gives you one plugin's team. Your agency gives them a dedicated security operation: multi-tool monitoring, proactive code audits, managed updates, human senior responders, guaranteed reinfection coverage. The gap between €100/mo and €500+/mo is where nobody competes with a productized offer. That's your market.
